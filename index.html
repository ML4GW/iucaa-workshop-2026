ML4GW+Controls Workshop
1-day Workshop on Machine Learning for Gravitational-Wave data analysis and controls.
About /Agenda /Lecture

ML4GW+Controls on Sept 25, 2026

This workshop introduces the tools, pipelines, and techniques at the intersection of machine learning and gravitational-wave science — from neural-network fundamentals to AI-assisted detector control.

Preceding the main workshop, there will be a keynote lecture on Sept 24, 2026 by Prof. Ajit Kembhavi.

Dates: Sept 24, 2026 - keynote lecture; Sept 25, 2026 - workshop

Location: IUCAA
