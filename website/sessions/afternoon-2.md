# Afternoon Session II — 15:30 to 18:00  — Sensing & Controls

This session will introduce participants to the essential components required to
intelligently sense and control a hardware system. Participants will assemble
a physical plant and integrate the necessary sensors and actuators. They will
then perform data-driven system identificant to develop a digital twin of the
system. Both conventional and neural-network-based controllers will be used
to drive the system to a desired operating point and maintain stable performance
under external disturbances. The session will also cover the deployment of
a neural-network on an embedded controller for real-time sensing and control.
